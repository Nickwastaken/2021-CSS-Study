Christmas is a holiday celebrated worldwide where people exchange gifts as a token of generosity. This holiday originated as the celebration of the birth of Jesus Christ, however most people don't celebrate the holiday for the religious portion. This is celebrated every 25th of December. The majority of the population celebrate this holiday by putting up a Christmas tree somewhere in their house and decorate the house, front lawn, driveway, and even the Christmas tree. When these people wake up, they go to the Christmas tree where "Santa" had put gifts. This holiday is a favorite by many people as everyone is usually happy and exchanging gifts to show their appreciation for one another.
